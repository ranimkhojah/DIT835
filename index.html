About Us

We live in a society that is pretty driven by consumerism. At the same time we are facing climate change and therefore, we are starting to care more about sustainable life. Therefore, we created Neighborhood Secondhand! A Free website to allow neighbors to give away goods of all kinds. The owners of goods shall be able to post stuff they want to give away (anything from furniture, to electronics, clothes and similar) and our customers (users of the website) would be able to catch such opportunities and get free stuff.
